What We Do

Parnassian Data Studio offers bespoke, broad-spectrum science solutions.

Data Engineering

Upstream of any modeling process is data collection, warehousing, and cleaning. We are adept at harvesting data via APIs or web scraping, transforming data into formats suitable for your applications, and making it accessible in thoughtfully architected databases.

Modeling

Data becomes valuable information through analysis and modeling. We specialize a wide variety of modeling techniques, including machine learning, mathematical statistics, Bayesian inference, and simulation.

User Interface

Even well-modeled data is of little value without a way for users to get at it. We build dashboards and fully-featured SaaS applications, complete with intuitive visualizations carefully selected for maximum insight.
